The First Date

Rhiannon and Phil initially met on Tinder. It took some convincing, but Phil managed to get Rhiannon to meet him for dinner at Toloache , on Manhattan's Upper East Side. They had instant chemistry, and after dinner went for drinks at Rathbone's Pub . There, Phil had his best idea ever: His favorite team, The New York Giants, were playing Rhiannon's favorite team, the Green Bay Packers, that weekend... They bet on the winner of the game, for dinner on their second date.

The Proposal

It took more than six years, but Phil was finally able to convince Rhiannon that they should get married. The evening of February 13th, not Valentine's Day, he invited her to join him in walking their dog, Freddy, and Rhiannon instantly knew something was happening. They walked to Carl Shurz Park , on the bank of the East River. It was a beautiful night, and the New York City skyline provided the backdrop for a moment neither will ever forget.
