Frequently Asked Questions

What precautions are being taken due to COVID-19?

We understand that concerns about COVID-19 are still top of mind for you, and we want to assure you that they are for us as well. As we think back on all that the last year has wrought for each of us, we're so excited to see thing changing for the better.

First, we'll both be fully vaccinated by June 5th, and we encourage everyone to get vaccinated themselves as well. There is no better step you can take to protect yourself and everyone with whom you interact. Had it been possible for us to enforce that all guests be fully vaccinated before attending, we would be doing just that.

Next, we want to make sure you're aware that the wedding ceremony will be held outdoors, but the reception will be held indoors. The venue's wedding event capacity is about 300 guests, so we have limited our guest list in order to reflect the size restrictions currently in place; you can expect fewer than 100 guests at our wedding.

Finally, we believe that masks will be required, so please be sure to bring one with you, and be prepared to wear it at all times. We cannot guarantee that we'll have one for you to wear should you leave yours at home. In the event that masks are no longer required by June 5th, we would still encourage you to wear one anyway.

We realize that our wedding falls at what still feels like the beginning of the turnaround, but we sincerely hope you can find a way to feel comfortable enough to attend. If you have any additional questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out using the form below.

Can I bring my children?

While we love your little ones, we want everyone to be able to relax and enjoy the evening. Our wedding is going to be an adults-only event, and we appreciate you making arrangements ahead of time so you can celebrate with us.

Can I bring a date?

Our wedding is strictly RSVP only. We will only able to accommodate those listed on your invitation.

Is there a dress code?

Cocktail attire, generally, but we want you to feel comfortable. Still, while we may be across the street from the beach, you should wear more than just your swimsuit.

Are there other wedding-related events I can attend?

Check out the Schedule for details about all wedding-related events.

Will the ceremony and reception be held indoors or outdoors?

Weather permitting, the ceremony will take place outdoors, on the Grand Hotel's sun deck, overlooking the beach. We've made arrangements for an indoor ceremony if the weather doesn't cooperate. The reception will take place indoors, in the atrium and ballroom.

What will the weather be like?

Hot and sunny, we hope! Early June in southern New Jersey typically falls in the low to mid-70s, without much humidity.
