The Grand Hotel of Cape May

1045 Beach Avenue
Cape May, New Jersey 08204
(609) 884 - 5611

This is where we've booked our room block for June 4th - 6th, and where both the ceremony and reception will take place. To receive a discount on your stay, mention that you are booking as part of the Lococo & Salant Wedding. If booking online, use the promo code 539765.

The Grand Hotel offers a variety of lodging accommodations including Double, Queen and King-size bedding. There are also several different suite options available, and ask about the newly renovated two-story Townhouse Suites! Resort amenities include olympic-size heated outdoor & indoor pools with a large Jacuzzi, bicycle rentals on site, professional fitness facility and more. Stay just steps from the beach and minutes from local boutique shops, quaint streets to tour, and casual eateries to feast.

ICONA Cape May

Joining some of America’s most charming and historic seaside motels and Victorian B&Bs, ICONA Cape May offers Cape May’s newest beachfront destination among hotels on the beach. The classic architecture and design, highlighted by a crisp, white façade and nautical blue shudders, calls to mind the shore getaways of yesteryear.

Directions to The Grand Hotel

From Philadelphia

Philadelphia International Airport (PHL) is the closest major airport, and a roughly 90 minute drive to Cape May. It is significantly closer to Cape May than the NYC airports.

  • Directions

From Atlantic City

Atlantic City International Airport (ACY) is closer to Cape May than Philadelphia, at a roughly 45 minute drive, but services fewer flights and airlines.

  • Directions

From New York City

For our guests traveling from New York City, we recommend either driving or taking a bus. You can expect the trip to take about three hours, but we promise it'll be worth it!
