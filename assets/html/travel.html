The Grand Hotel of Cape May

1045 Beach Avenue
Cape May, New Jersey 08204
(609) 884 - 5611

This is where we've booked our room block for June 4th - 6th, and where both the ceremony and reception will take place. More details our room block coming soon.

The Grand Hotel offers a variety of lodging accommodations including Double, Queen and King-size bedding, some with efficiency units included. There are also several different suite options available, and ask about the newly renovated Two Story Townhouse Suites! Resort amenities include an olympic size heated outdoor & heated indoor pool with large Jacuzzi, bicycle rentals on site, professional fitness facility, family fun game room and more. Stay just steps from the beach and minutes from local boutique shops, quaint streets to tour, and casual eateries to feast.

Directions to The Grand Hotel

From Philadelphia

Philadelphia International Airport (PHL) is the closest major airport, and a roughly 90 minute drive to Cape May. It is significantly closer to Cape May than the New York City airports.

  • Directions

From Atlantic City

Atlantic City International Airport (ACY) is closer to Cape May than Philadelphia, at a roughly 45 minute drive, but services fewer flights and airlines.

  • Directions

From New York City

For our guests traveling from New York City, we recommend either driving or taking a bus. You can expect the trip to take about three hours, but we promise it'll be worth it!
