The People

The names and faces that will make the day unforgettable.

Wedding Party

Jena Lococo

Maid of Honor

Jena is Rhiannon's younger sister and they've been the best of friends for as long as either can remember. Their shared love of traveling has lead them on some exciting adventures - from zorbing in New Zealand, to safariing in Kenya.

Jesse Marino

Best Man

Jesse and Phil met during the summer after 4th grade at LakeView Day Camp, making him Phil's oldest friend. Jesse is always quick with a joke, a drink, or a slap to the face. As Phil's best man, he'll likely provide several of each.

Marisa Franceour

Marisa and Rhiannon met during their sophomore year of high school and have been best friends ever since. Although they haven't lived in the same place for the last 13 years, they still keep each other laughing constantly.

Beau Salant

Beau is Phil's younger brother, and currently resides in Washington, D.C. where he works in political polling. With a plethora of knowledge of all things movies, theater, and politics, Beau is never one to back down from a debate.

Olivia Murphy

After moving to NYC in 2013, Rhiannon was lucky to meet fellow New York newbie, Oliva. They quickly realized that their outlooks on life and ways of thinking were identical. Olivia is one of the most intelligent and giving people Rhiannon knows.

Jon Noah

Jon and Phil first met in high school, and immortalized their friendship after spending a year as roommates in New York City. Somehow even after a year of living together, they remain close friends.

Paige Rayman

Paige and Rhiannon have enjoyed being nerdy together since high school. Their shared love of Harry Potter, math, and french class bonded them from the beginning. Paige is a thoughtful friend who always remembers the little things.

Carter Malmo

Carter and Phil met just a few years ago, but became fast friends after bonding over things like football, Game of Thrones, and a good nitro stout from the deli. Despite being a talented guitarist, Carter still can't seem to find a drummer for his band.

Melissa Waggy

Melissa and Rhiannon met in high school, where they enjoyed spending many weekends at Melissa's watching Friends on repeat. Melissa has always been Rhiannon's favorite person to ask for advice, because she always tells you exactly what she thinks.

Ben Rosen

Ben and Phil became friends in high school, and bonded over their mutual love of football, politics, and giving Jesse a hard time. Nobody is as content as Phil to watch as much football on Sundays as possible, except perhaps for Ben.
